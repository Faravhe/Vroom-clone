vroom24x7.net is our sell/buy portal
Just go to the portal and you will find hundreds of vehicles waiting for you to explore. Our aim is bring onboard all leading auto dealers and business houses so that you can have ample options to select from. You can select vehicle type, location and brand wise from the menu. Detail information on the vehicle will be shown in an intuitive manner.
Services that you need while buying & selling
Vroom offers the complete suite of services that one needs to complete the vehicle purchase. Inspection service at home and workshop, ownership transfer service, buying insurance, applying bank loan and even regular maintenance like wash and polish can be ordered from a single platform. Just browse through the site and check for yourself the array of services bring at your mouse-click.
Bidding and booking money
We are introducing two new ways to sell/buy vehicles. Individual sellers will receive bid offers through email when they post any listing. No more unwanted phone calls. From the offers received, s/he will call to finally negotiate. And auto dealers will be able to take booking money against their listings so that only genuine buyers are there and they get the preferential service.
